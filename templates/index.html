Brain cancer remains one of the most challenging conditions to detect and treat effectively. Timely and accurate diagnosis plays a critical role in improving patient outcomes. Our project harnesses advanced medical imaging and cutting-edge technology to revolutionize tumor detection and monitoring. Additionally, our predictive analytics feature estimates the tumor's growth over the next month, empowering medical professionals with the information needed to plan proactive and personalized treatments. Detect tumor using IRM

This program is designed to read Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) scans and identify the presence and location of tumors. Leveraging advanced image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms, it provides accurate and efficient tumor detection to assist medical professionals in diagnosis and treatment planning.

3D model

This program processes a sequence of MRI image slices to reconstruct a 3D visualization of detected tumors. It provides medical professionals with a comprehensive spatial understanding of the tumor's shape, size, and position within the body, enhancing diagnosis and treatment planning.

Volume Prediction System

This program analyzes two sets of MRI images taken at different time points and predicts the tumor's volume after 15 days. Using advanced machine learning models trained on temporal medical imaging data, the program estimates the tumor's growth or shrinkage, providing valuable insights for treatment planning and monitoring.
